Introduction


High Point University's campus is not an easy place to traverse, and it is especially overwhelming to newer students and visitors. This app was developed to help people find their way to class, food locations, and more.


Overview


Group Size: 4

Software: Android Studio

Language: Java

Main Menu


The Main Menu screen consists of 2 spinners of different locations around camppus. The first will choose the starting locations and the second will choose their destination. Once both locations are chosen a Google Maps activity will open.

Maps Activity


This Google Maps activity will open up after 2 locations are chosen and will pull up a satellite view of campus. Two markers will be placed on the map with the red being the current location and the purple being the destination.
