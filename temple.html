Project Details


Engine and Tools: Adventure Game Studio | Aseprite | Clip Studio Paint

Language: C++

Team Size: Solo Project

Duration: 12 weeks

Puzzle #1


Problem: Can't get past the barricade of logs, must find a way through!


Solution: By picking up the mossy rock and the stick, the player is able to combine items in their inventory to craft an axe to break through the barricade.

Puzzle #2


Problem: The warrior guarding the temple won't let you go any further, find a way to prove yourself worthy.


Solution: Show your grandfather's medallion to the warrior in order to pass.

Puzzle #3


Problem: The entrance to the temple is locked, find a way to open it!


Solution: Find the 3 gems scattered throughout the world and place them in their correct slots to open the gate.
